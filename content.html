About GITAM University

GITAM (Gandhi Institute of Technology and Management) University is a premier educational institution located in Visakhapatnam, Andhra Pradesh. Established in 1980, GITAM has become synonymous with quality education, offering undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs across various fields including Engineering, Science, Management, Pharmacy, and Humanities.

The Visakhapatnam campus is renowned for its state-of-the-art facilities, experienced faculty, and vibrant student community. The lush green campus by the Bay of Bengal provides a perfect setting for academic excellence and holistic development.

GITAM is committed to fostering innovation, research, and entrepreneurship among its students, making it a top choice for aspiring learners across India.
